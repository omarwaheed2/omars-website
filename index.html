Objective

The world of Software Engineering has always intrigued me. While in university, never did I know, there are things other than just writing code. Designing complex systems, maintaining large code-base, scaling systems , writing code that is clear and concise. When all of these things combine, we get what we call a Software Engineer. These are also the things that I strive to learn and get better at. My objective is to keep my learning continued and skills updated, while side by side applying the newest and cutting edge skills to solve tech related problems in a creative way.

What I Do ?

I am currently a Software Engineer in Market Data & News Team at the headquarter of Factset Research Systems. My team owns the backend for all news related apps in our FactSet terminal. My team's tech stack includes, Python, Java and Perl with a variety of databases such as SwiftStack, ElasticSearch, MySql. I have developed a bunch of large scale cloud-based web-services over my years at Factset and have also taken ownership for most of them. Other than this I have also contributed in other parts such as creating tools and automation scripts for increasing developer productivity across teams.
